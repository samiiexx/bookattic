About Us

We are a global community established to support the sustainable development of youth, women, minority and vulnerable communities through human capacity development, impact investment and innovation.

What we Stand for

A non-profit organisation created to drive social sustainability and inclusion on the continent, leveraging on human-centred design for impact. Our work is built on the principle of social sustainability that focuses on youth, women and vulnerable communities, overcoming the barriers that prevent them from fully participating in society, and supporting their efforts to create and shape their future.
